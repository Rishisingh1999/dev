Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about MemoryFrame

How long can videos be?
Any length up to your plan's storage limit. Recommend 1-10 minutes for the best mobile experience. Most memories work best between 30 seconds and 5 minutes.
Can I edit or delete a memory?
Yes. You have full control over all memories. Edit titles, replace videos, or delete anytime. QR codes will immediately reflect changes.
What video formats are supported?
MP4, WebM, MOV, MKV, AVI, and more. If your format doesn't work, we'll convert it automatically for optimal streaming.
Is there a storage limit?
Free: 100 MB per memory. Pro: 1 GB per memory. Business: 5 GB per memory. Plenty of space for your most precious moments.
Can I share memories with password protection?
Yes! Pro and Business plans include password protection. Perfect for sensitive family videos or private moments.
Do viewers need an account?
Not at all. They just scan the QR code or click the link. Works instantly on any phone, no apps, no signup, no friction.
How secure is my data?
Military-grade encryption. Your videos are stored securely and are never sold, analyzed, or shared. You own your memories completely.
Can I print the QR codes?
Absolutely! Print unlimited QR codes at any size. Stick them in albums, on photo cards, or anywhere you want to add that interactive moment.
