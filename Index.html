How MemoryFrame works
3 steps
1. Upload a video.
Drop any short clip (anniversary, trip, graduation) into your private MemoryFrame dashboard.
2. Get a unique link & QR.
MemoryFrame creates a secure link and auto‑generates a QR code ready for printing next to your photo.
3. Scan the photo.
Anyone who scans the QR opens a clean web page that plays the linked video on top of that memory.

Built on Supabase + static HTML. You keep full control over your data, domains, and deployment.
